THE SPECIALITY COFFEE

Roastery Coffee House was founded with one goal - We wanted to brew India's finest speciality coffee and share it with as many people as possible.
We source and roast some of the best beans available in our country. All our coffees have a score of 80 and above. Besides our signature estates, we are always on a quest to procure interesting coffee from farms across India.

At Roastery, we know we owe it all to our farmers. We have the deepest gratitude for them. When we started in 2017, Indian speciality coffee was not very popular and we didn't have a coffee culture yet. Despite being underpaid, our farmers were heavily dependent on exports. The first time we went to source beans, nobody had heard about Roastery — we were brand new. In a market where beans are only sold in bulk, we worked closely with our partners to create better returns for growers and build a fairer supply chain.

Our team makes multiple farm visits every year. Meanwhile, we are regularly in touch with our friends at the farm even from afar. The shared love and passion for Indian speciality coffee helps us bond as learners more than anything.

FROM INDIA TO THE WORLD

With a coffee culture now alive and kicking in India, coffee farmers have benefited more than ever. Right now, India's own demand for its coffee is on the rise like never before. Our farmers now prefer to sell their coffee beans to Indian companies. Exports have drastically reduced.
